## Design Context

### Users
- **Locals**: Regulars looking for the daily specials or to book their favorite table.
- **Tourists**: Visitors to the medieval town of Mirepoix seeking an authentic "sudiste" dining experience (pizza, crêpes, local products). Often on mobile, in daylight.
- **Families & Groups**: Looking for a convivial and warm atmosphere with generous portions.

### Brand Personality
- **Warm & Authentic**: A place that feels like home, run by passionate owners (Davy & Julie).
- **Sudiste**: Deeply rooted in the South of France, reflecting the warmth of the Ariège region.
- **3-Word Personality**: Earthy, Convivial, Artisanal.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: "Bistrot Editorial" — the warmth of a French bistro translated through a magazine lens. Photography-forward, generous whitespace, typography-driven.
- **Theme**: Light/Cream base (tourists on phones in daylight). Warm terracotta/brick accents.
- **Quality Bar**: Flagship polish (not MVP) with detail-first finishing pass.
- **Primary Emotion**: Convivialite (welcoming, warm, human).
- **Accessibility Target**: Light-only UI with reinforced WCAG AA (contrast and focus states prioritized).
- **Palette (OKLCH)**:
  - Brand: `oklch(0.55 0.14 30)` — deep terracotta
  - Brand warm: `oklch(0.62 0.12 45)` — warm hover accent
  - Surface: `oklch(0.97 0.005 75)` — cream base
  - Surface raised: `oklch(0.99 0.003 75)` — cards
  - Surface deep: `oklch(0.93 0.01 60)` — alternating sections
  - Ink: `oklch(0.25 0.015 50)` — primary text
  - Ink muted: `oklch(0.45 0.01 50)` — secondary text
  - Olive: `oklch(0.55 0.06 140)` — nature accent
- **Typography**:
  - Display: Libre Caslon Display (transitional serif, old printing press warmth)
  - Body: Source Sans 3 (humanist sans, warm and readable)
  - Accent: Anybody (geometric sans with handmade quality, labels and prices)

### Design Principles
1. **Bistrot Editorial**: Treat every section like a magazine spread — generous scale, intentional whitespace, photography as the primary content.
2. **Materiality & Depth**: Subtle shadows, layered surfaces, and warm cream textures create a "tactile" feel without flatness.
3. **Heritage Typography**: Large-scale Libre Caslon Display headings with tight leading evoke a high-end bistro chalkboard menu.
4. **Mobile-First Utility**: Menu and reservation phone are accessible with one thumb. Floating CTA on mobile ensures conversion at any scroll depth.
5. **Translation-Ready**: All strings use `__()` / `_e()`. Layout accommodates text expansion for WPML/Polylang.
